I WANT TO GO!

Charlotte's Data Hackathon

Charlotte's Data Hackathon

Clear your calendar for the Data Hackathon at HQ Charlotte! You're invited to take part in the event as a participant, or just to watch.

Track 1: Developers - we'll provide the tools for you to build a connector to the Stratifyd text analytics platform. Analyze any text, even in unstructured format like blogs, customer comments, social media, email, you name it! Judges will grant prizes for the best demos!

Track 2: Problem Solvers - don't worry if you are not a developer. We will provide a free user account to the Stratifyd text analytics platform, where you can utilize existing data connectors, or upload an open data source file to solve a problem. Judges will grant prizes for the best problem solvers!

Snacks and drinks provided!

Just want to watch? That's OK too!

Bring a guest, and enjoy the event. This is going to be awesome!

The Location

HQ Charlotte at Packard Place

222 South Church St. Charlotte, NC

Located at the corner of S. Church St. and Third St., HQ Charlotte is one of the largest entrepreneurship centers in Charlotte. We'll hold the main sessions in the Packard Place 'garage' space, with breakout rooms available for the working teams.
